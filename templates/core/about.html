About MindTrack

Transforming mental health assessment management for healthcare professionals worldwide.

Our Mission

At MindTrack, our mission is to simplify the process of mental health assessments, making it easier for healthcare professionals to collect, analyze, and act on patient data.

We believe that by streamlining these processes, we can help improve patient outcomes, reduce administrative burden on healthcare providers, and ultimately contribute to better mental health care worldwide.

Our Story

MindTrack was founded in 2022 by a team of healthcare professionals and software engineers who recognized the challenges in managing mental health assessments efficiently.

After witnessing firsthand the administrative burden that paper-based questionnaires and manual scoring created, our founders set out to build a solution that would make the entire process digital, automated, and more effective.

Today, MindTrack is used by thousands of healthcare professionals across multiple countries, helping them save time, reduce errors, and provide better care to their patients.
